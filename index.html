About

Meet Aaron

Hi, I'm Aaron. I'm a classically trained tenor with a Bachelor of Music in Vocal Performance from the University of North Florida. I have performed in operas including Mozart's The Magic Flute, singing Tamino, and Strauss's Die Fledermaus as Dr. Blind, and I have had the joy of singing at The Vatican.

My focus now is teaching. I work one on one with each singer to build strong, healthy technique rooted in the bel canto tradition, the Italian classical style built on beautiful, sustainable tone. Whether you want to sing opera, musical theatre, or today's pop, the foundation is the same: a free, easy, well-supported voice that lasts.

Singers of every age and level are welcome here, from first-timers to performers preparing for an audition. Let's find your voice together.
